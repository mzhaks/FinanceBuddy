**Text Summary:**
- DAP is defined as a registered and logged-in user of Meta's "Family" products (Facebook, Instagram, Messenger, WhatsApp) visiting at least one product daily via mobile app or browser.
- DAP numbers do not include users on other products unless they qualify based on Family product activity.
- DAP calculations use complex techniques, algorithms, and machine learning models to estimate unique people, matching user accounts within and across products.
- Models are developed based on internal reviews and calibrated against user survey data.
- An update in Q3'22 contributed approximately 30 million DAP to reported worldwide figures in September 2022.
- Beginning in Q4'23, Family metrics no longer include Messenger Kids users.
