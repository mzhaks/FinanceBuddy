**Chart Analysis: Advertising Revenue by User Geography (In Millions)**

*   **Revenue by Region (Q1'23 - Lowest Quarter):**

**Significant Trends:**

*   **Overall Revenue:** Exhibits strong growth from Q1'23 ($28,101M) to Q4'24 ($46,783M). There's a noticeable seasonal dip in Q1 of both 2023 and 2024 following strong Q4 performance.
*   **US & Canada:** Consistently the largest revenue contributor, showing substantial growth from $12,710M (Q1'23) to $20,982M (Q4'24).
*   **Europe:** The second-largest contributor, also displaying a clear upward trend from $6,269M (Q1'23) to $11,154M (Q4'24).
*   **Asia-Pacific:** Shows consistent growth throughout the period, from $5,893M (Q1'23) to $9,012M (Q4'24).
*   **Rest of World:** Maintains steady growth, increasing from $3,229M (Q1'23) to $5,635M (Q4'24).
*   All regions demonstrate a similar seasonal pattern, with lower revenue in Q1 following a strong Q4.

**Text Summary (Footnote):**
Revenue by user geography is estimated based on the geographic location of users performing revenue-generating activities. This differs from revenue disclosures in consolidated financial statements, which apportion revenue based on customer addresses.
