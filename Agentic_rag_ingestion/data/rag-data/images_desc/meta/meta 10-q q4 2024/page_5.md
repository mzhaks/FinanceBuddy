**Significant Trends:**

*   **Overall Expense Reduction:** Total expenses as a percentage of revenue show a strong declining trend, from 80% in Q4'22 to 52% in Q4'24. The sharpest decline occurred between Q2'23 (71%) and Q3'23 (59%).
*   **Cost of Revenue:** Decreased from 26% in Q4'22 to stabilize around 18-19% from Q3'23 onwards.
*   **Research & Development (R&D):** Peaked at 33% in Q1'23, then gradually decreased to 25% in Q4'24, remaining the largest expense category.
*   **Marketing & Sales:** Declined from 14% in Q4'22 to consistently hover around 7-8% from Q3'23 to Q4'24.
*   **General & Administrative (G&A):** Shows the most substantial reduction, falling from 10-13% in Q4'22-Q2'23 to a low of 2% in Q4'24.
