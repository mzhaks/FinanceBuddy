**Text Summary:**

*   **DAP Definition:** A registered and logged-in user of Facebook, Instagram, Messenger, and/or WhatsApp (Family products) who visited at least one product via mobile app or web/mobile browser on a given day.
*   **Exclusions:** DAP numbers do not include users on other products unless they qualify based on Family product activities.
*   **Calculation Method:** DAP calculations use complex techniques, algorithms, and machine learning models to estimate unique individuals across products, attributing multiple user accounts to one person. These methods involve significant judgment and a margin of error.
*   **Reporting Change:** Beginning Q4'23, Family metrics no longer include Messenger Kids users.
