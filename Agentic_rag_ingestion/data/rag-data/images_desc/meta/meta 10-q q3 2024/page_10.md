**Text Summary:**
- DAP is defined as a registered and logged-in user of Facebook, Instagram, Messenger, and/or WhatsApp (Family products) visiting via mobile device or web browser on a given day.
- DAP calculation uses complex techniques, algorithms, and machine learning models to estimate unique people across multiple products, calibrated against user survey data, with a margin of error.
- In Q3 2022, an update to Family metrics calculations contributed approximately 30 million DAP to reported worldwide DAP.
- Beginning in Q4 2023, Family metrics no longer include Messenger Kids users.
