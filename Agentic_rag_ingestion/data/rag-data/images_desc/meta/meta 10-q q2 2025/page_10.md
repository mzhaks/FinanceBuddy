**Text Summary:**

*   DAP is defined as a registered, logged-in user of Facebook, Instagram, Messenger, and/or WhatsApp who visited at least one Family product via mobile app or web/mobile browser on a given day.
*   DAP numbers do not include users on other products unless they qualify based on other Family product activities.
*   DAP calculations estimate unique people across multiple products using complex techniques, algorithms, machine learning, and matching user accounts. This involves significant judgment and has a margin of error, calibrated against user survey data.
*   Starting Q4'23, Family metrics no longer include Messenger Kids users.
